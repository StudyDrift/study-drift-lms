## 1. Problem Statement

Self-learners and college students frequently lack metacognitive skills — they do not know when they are studying effectively, how to allocate study time across topics, or whether their current study approach is working. Lextures collects engagement and performance data but never surfaces it to students in a motivational, actionable self-coaching format. Competitors like Khan Academy and Duolingo have proven that gamified study-skills feedback dramatically improves retention and course completion. Without a self-reflection dashboard, Lextures is analytically passive on the student side.

## 2. Goals

- Surface each student's own engagement metrics (time on task, login streak, video watch trends) in a motivational self-reflection view.
- Provide personalized, AI-assisted weekly study coaching tips derived from their behavioral patterns.
- Include a simple self-reflection journaling component where students can log how study sessions felt.
- Show a study-time allocation chart helping students see if they are spending time on low-mastery topics.

## 3. Non-Goals

- Mental health assessment or clinical wellbeing screening (outside scope; would require counseling integration).
- Mandating student self-reflection (opt-in feature; instructors and students can disable it).
- Replacing the instructor-facing at-risk dashboard (9.2) — this is a student-facing complement.

## 4. Personas & User Stories

- **As a self-learner studying for a certification**, I want to see how many hours I studied this week compared to my goal so that I can adjust my schedule before the exam.
- **As a college student**, I want my dashboard to show that I keep skipping Module 4 so that I can confront my avoidance behavior.
- **As a student**, I want a weekly AI tip like "You study best on Tuesday mornings — try scheduling your hardest topics then" so that I can study more strategically.
- **As a self-learner**, I want to write a quick note after each study session ("felt confused about recursion today") so that I can track patterns in my own understanding.

## 5. Functional Requirements

- **FR-1.** The student dashboard MUST show a "Study Stats" widget with: current weekly streak (days with ≥ 1 login), total time on task this week, and goal vs. actual comparison if a study goal is set.
- **FR-2.** The dashboard MUST display a time-allocation chart showing study minutes per module/topic over the last 14 days.
- **FR-3.** The system MUST generate a weekly AI coaching tip per student using their engagement summary and quiz performance as context, delivered via in-app notification and email (6.2).
- **FR-4.** Students MUST be able to set a weekly study goal (hours per week) and see progress toward it.
- **FR-5.** The dashboard MUST include a lightweight journal: students can add a brief (≤ 280 character) reflection note after each session; stored privately, visible only to the student.
- **FR-6.** The coaching tip AI MUST use `student_notebook_rag_ai.rs` patterns — query against the student's recent performance and engagement, not course-level data.
- **FR-7.** The system MUST track "study efficiency" — the ratio of quiz score improvement to time on task — and flag when a student is spending a lot of time but not improving (suggesting a change in study strategy).
- **FR-8.** Students MUST be able to opt out of the self-reflection feature; opt-out suppresses the dashboard widget and stops AI tip generation.

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

- **Performance** — Dashboard widget loads < 500 ms (served from cached engagement summaries). AI coaching tip generated asynchronously; displayed when ready (async notification).
- **Security** — Self-reflection journal entries are private to the student; no instructor can read them. Coaching tips generated with anonymized context (no raw quiz responses to LLM — aggregate metrics only).
- **Privacy & Compliance** — FERPA: journal entries and coaching tips are student-generated/consumed records; not accessible by school officials under standard FERPA provisions. GDPR: opt-out + erasure. AI tip context must not include PII sent to external LLM without consent (per 10.17).
- **Accessibility** — Dashboard widget keyboard-navigable; streak indicator uses text not just icon. Journal textarea properly labeled. WCAG 2.1 AA.
- **Scalability** — AI coaching tip generated once per student per week (nightly batch); not per-request.
- **Reliability** — If AI tip generation fails, skip the tip for that week; do not surface an error to the student.
- **Observability** — Metrics: `coaching_tips_generated_total`, `journal_entries_created_total`, `study_goal_set_pct`. Log student_id (hashed), course_id, tip_generated=true/false.
- **Maintainability** — Coaching tip generation in `server/src/services/ai/coaching_tips.rs` (new); dashboard widget in `clients/web/src/pages/lms/Dashboard.tsx`.
- **Internationalization** — AI coaching tip generated in the student's interface language. Goal hours format locale-aware.
- **Backward compatibility** — Opt-in widget; students who do not enable it see no change to their dashboard.

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

- **AC-1.** *Given* a student has logged in 5 consecutive days *When* they view their dashboard *Then* the streak widget shows "5-day streak 🔥" (or equivalent text for screen readers: "5-day study streak").
- **AC-2.** *Given* a student sets a goal of 10 hours/week and studies 7 hours by Thursday *When* they view the dashboard *Then* the goal bar shows "7 of 10 hours — 3 more to reach your goal this week."
- **AC-3.** *Given* a student's engagement data shows they study most on Tuesdays and Thursdays *When* the weekly AI coaching tip is generated *Then* the tip references their optimal study days and suggests scheduling difficult topics on those days.
- **AC-4.** *Given* a student writes a journal entry "Confused about recursion" *When* they log out and log back in *Then* their journal shows the entry with the date; no instructor can retrieve the entry via any API.
- **AC-5.** *Given* a student opts out of self-reflection *When* they view the dashboard *Then* the study stats widget and journal are hidden and no coaching tips are generated or emailed.
- **AC-6.** *Given* an AI coaching tip is generated for a student *When* the tip is stored *Then* the raw tip context sent to the LLM contains only aggregate metrics (avg_time_s, login_count, avg_quiz_score) — no student name, email, or quiz response text.

## 8. Data Model

```sql
-- server/migrations/092_self_reflection.sql
CREATE TABLE analytics.study_goals (
  user_id         UUID PRIMARY KEY REFERENCES user_account(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  weekly_hours    REAL NOT NULL,
  opted_in        BOOLEAN NOT NULL DEFAULT true,
  created_at      TIMESTAMPTZ NOT NULL DEFAULT now()
);

CREATE TABLE analytics.reflection_journal (
  id              UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  user_id         UUID NOT NULL REFERENCES user_account(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  course_id       UUID REFERENCES course.courses(id),
  entry_text      TEXT NOT NULL CHECK (char_length(entry_text) <= 280),
  created_at      TIMESTAMPTZ NOT NULL DEFAULT now()
);
CREATE INDEX ON analytics.reflection_journal (user_id, created_at DESC);

CREATE TABLE analytics.coaching_tips (
  id              UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  user_id         UUID NOT NULL REFERENCES user_account(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  tip_text        TEXT NOT NULL,
  week_of         DATE NOT NULL,
  delivered_at    TIMESTAMPTZ,
  UNIQUE (user_id, week_of)
);
```

## 9. API Surface

| Method | Path | Auth | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| `GET` | `/me/study-stats` | JWT (self) | Returns streak, time this week, goal progress |
| `PUT` | `/me/study-goal` | JWT (self) | Set or update weekly hour goal and opt-in status |
| `GET` | `/me/reflection-journal` | JWT (self) | Paginated journal entries |
| `POST` | `/me/reflection-journal` | JWT (self) | Create journal entry |
| `DELETE` | `/me/reflection-journal/:id` | JWT (self) | Delete entry |
| `GET` | `/me/coaching-tips` | JWT (self) | Latest coaching tip |

All `/me/` endpoints are self-only — no cross-user access at the route layer.

## 10. UI / UX

- Student dashboard (`Dashboard.tsx`): new "Study Stats" card with streak counter, weekly progress bar, time-allocation bar chart.
- "My Goals" settings: weekly hour slider, opt-in toggle for coaching tips.
- Journal widget: expandable textarea at the bottom of each content page ("How did this feel? (optional)"); saved journal visible on the Study Stats page.
- Weekly coaching tip: displayed as a banner card on the dashboard with a lightbulb icon and personalized text. Dismiss button. Link to full insights page.
- Insights page (new route `/me/study-insights`): full study stats history, journal archive, coaching tip history.
- Accessibility: streak counter uses `aria-label="5-day study streak"`; progress bar `role="progressbar"`; journal textarea has `aria-label`.
- Mobile: all widgets designed for 360px viewport; journal textarea full-width.
- i18n keys: `study.streak`, `study.goal`, `study.tip`, `study.journal`, `study.optIn`, `study.timeAllocation`.

## 11. AI / ML Considerations

- **Model:** OpenRouter-routed LLM (existing pattern in `server/src/services/ai/`).
- **Prompt pattern:** Weekly coaching tip prompt uses student's aggregate metrics as context (avg_daily_time, login_count, weak_topics from mastery heatmap, score_trend). No PII, no raw quiz responses.
- **Eval metric:** weekly tip usefulness rating (student thumbs up/down, stored for evaluation).
- **Fallback:** if LLM fails, serve a generic tip from a pre-written pool of 20 study-skills tips.
- **Cost budget:** 1 tip per student per week; ~200 tokens per request; marginal cost per user/week.
- **PII redaction:** aggregate context only; per 10.17 AI disclosure.

## 14. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI coaching tips are generic or unhelpful | H | M | A/B test tip quality; collect thumbs up/down; fallback to curated tip pool |
| Journal entries misinterpreted as mental health crisis signals | L | H | No AI processing of journal text; purely student-private. No clinical assessment |
| Students ignore opt-in prompt and miss the feature | M | M | Onboarding flow surfaces the goal-setting step; re-prompt after first quiz |
| Streak gamification causes anxiety in struggling students | M | M | Freeze streak counter if student is at-risk (9.2 flag); frame as progress, not performance |

## 15. Rollout Plan

- **Flag:** `FEATURE_SELF_REFLECTION=true` (default false; student opt-in within the feature).
- **Sequencing:** DB migrations → engagement data dependency → study stats API → dashboard widget → journal → AI coaching tip (last, requires LLM) → enable flag.
- **GA criteria:** streak and goal progress accurate; AI tip generated for >90 % of opted-in students per week; journal private from all APIs.
- **Rollback:** disable flag; journal entries retained in DB.

## 16. Test Plan

- **Unit** — Streak calculation logic; study efficiency ratio; weekly hours accumulation.
- **Integration** — Engagement summaries → study stats API: correct streak and goal progress.
- **End-to-end** — Playwright: set goal, simulate 3 days of engagement events, verify streak and progress bar.
- **Security** — Confirm instructor cannot read journal entries (403); confirm coaching tip context contains no PII.
- **Accessibility** — axe scan of dashboard widget; screen-reader announcement of streak.
- **AI testing** — 5 representative student profiles → tip generation → manual review for relevance and safety.

## 17. Documentation & Training
