# Lextures Pricing

## Overview

Lextures pricing is designed to let individual instructors get started for free and scale to paid only when they are running real courses at real volume.

---

## Free Tier

| Limit | Value |
|---|---|
| Students per course | Up to 30 |
| Courses per instructor | Up to 5 |
| Price | $0 forever |

The free tier is not a trial. There is no time limit and no credit card required. An instructor teaching a single section of 28 students never needs to pay anything.

---

## Education Tier

| | |
|---|---|
| **Price** | $9.99 per enrolled student / year |
| **Courses** | Unlimited |
| **Students** | Unlimited |

Billing is based on peak enrollment across all courses in a rolling 12-month window. A student enrolled in multiple courses taught by the same instructor counts as one student.

### Example

An instructor with 3 courses and 90 unique enrolled students pays:

```
90 students × $9.99 = $897.10 / year
```

## FAQ

**Does a student enrolled in two of my courses count twice?**
No. Each unique student counts once regardless of how many of your courses they are in.

**What happens if I exceed 30 students in a free-tier course?**
Enrollment above 30 will prompt an upgrade to Pro. Existing students are not removed.

**What happens if I exceed 5 courses on the free tier?**
Creating a 6th course prompts an upgrade. Existing courses and their data are unaffected.

**Is there institutional or district pricing?**
Contact us. Large deployments (500+ students) are negotiated separately.

**Is Lextures still open source?**
Yes. Self-hosted deployments under AGPL-3.0 are free without these limits. The pricing above applies to the hosted service at lextures.com.
