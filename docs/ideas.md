# Lextures

This application suite is known as a University as a Service, where many aspects of a university is automated.

## Areas of Automation

 - Student Records & Registration
     - Academic Catalog
     - Academic Calendar
     - Student Enrollments
     - Degree Audit
     - Section assignments
     - Course approval and management
     - Substitutions and waivers
     - Transcripts
 - Admissions
     - Application requests
     - Student tracking
 - Human Resources
 - Financial Aid
 - Career Center
 - Information Technology
 - Student support
 - Tutoring
 - Advising
     - Graduation Planner


## Common Areas of Technology

 - Workflow management (ability to add a custom workflow). Use react flow or n8n
