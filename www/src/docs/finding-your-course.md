Welcome to Lextures! Whether you're a new student or an instructor, getting to your course content should be the easiest part of your day. Follow this simple 3-step guide to get started.

## Step 1: Sign In to Your Account
Head over to the [Lextures Sign-in Page](http://localhost:5173/login). Use the email address and password provided by your institution.

![Lextures Login Page](/docs-login.png)

> [!TIP]
> If your institution uses Single Sign-On (SSO), look for the "Sign in with Google" or "Sign in with Microsoft" buttons to bypass the manual password entry.

---

## Step 2: Locate the "My Courses" Section
After logging in, you'll land on your personal **Dashboard**. This is your mission control for everything happening in Lextures.

Look for the section titled **"My Courses"**. All your active enrollments will be displayed here as high-visibility cards.

![Lextures Dashboard My Courses](/docs-dashboard.png)

Each card provides a snapshot of:
- **Course Name**: e.g., "Advanced AI Ethics"
- **Instructor**: The lead teacher for that section.
- **Your Progress**: A visual bar showing how much of the curriculum you've completed.

---

## Step 3: Enter Your Course
Simply **click anywhere on the course card** to enter the learning environment. 

Once inside, you'll have immediate access to:
- **The Syllabus**: Your roadmap for the term.
- **Assignments**: Upcoming tasks and deadlines.
- **Grades**: Real-time feedback on your performance.

---

## Troubleshooting: "Where is my course?"

If your dashboard looks empty, don't panic! Try these steps:

1.  **Check Your Email**: Ensure you've received an enrollment confirmation for the specific course.
2.  **Verify Your Account**: Double-check that you are signed in with the correct institutional email address.
3.  **Refresh Your Session**: Sometimes a quick page refresh (`Cmd+R` or `Ctrl+R`) is all it takes to sync the latest roster data.
4.  **Contact Support**: If you still don't see your course, reach out to your instructor or open a ticket via the [Help Center](/#/docs).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Courses usually appear on your dashboard 24-48 hours before the official start date. If your term hasn't started yet, your course might be in the "Upcoming" state.
