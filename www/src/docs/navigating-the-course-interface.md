Once you've entered a course, you'll find a consistent, focused environment designed to help you stay on track. Here's a breakdown of the key areas you'll interact with.

## The Navigation Sidebar
Located on the left side of the screen, the sidebar is your primary way to move between different types of course activities.

![Lextures Course Interface](/docs-course-interface.png)

- **Lessons**: This is where you'll spend most of your time. Lessons contain videos, readings, and interactive content.
- **Assignments**: View upcoming deadlines and submit your work.
- **Discussions**: Engage with your peers and instructors on course topics.
- **Grades**: Track your performance and read feedback from your instructor.
- **Calendar**: See a visual schedule of all your course events and due dates.

> [!NOTE]
> Some features may be enabled or disabled by your instructor depending on the specific needs of the course.

---

## The Lesson View
When you select a lesson, the content occupies the main area of the screen.

1.  **Header**: Displays the lesson title and your current progress within that specific module.
2.  **Content Area**: Features the primary learning material, which may include embedded videos, text, or interactive simulations.
3.  **Resources Sidebar**: Quick links to supplemental materials, such as PDF readings or external websites.
4.  **Navigation Buttons**: Use the **"Next"** and **"Previous"** buttons at the bottom of the page to move through the content at your own pace.

---

## Submitting Your Work
Assignments in Lextures are designed to be straightforward. When you're ready to submit:

1.  Navigate to the **Assignments** tab.
2.  Select the relevant assignment from the list.
3.  Follow the prompts to upload files or enter text directly.
4.  Click the **Submit** button to finalize your entry.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always wait for the "Submission Successful" confirmation message before closing your browser. You will also receive a confirmation email for your records.

---

## Getting Help in Real-time
If you run into technical issues or have questions about the material:

- **Help Widget**: Click the **Help icon** in the bottom right corner for contextual guides.
- **Messaging**: Use the **Inbox** (found in the main platform navigation) to send a direct message to your instructor.
