Creating a new course in Lextures is an intuitive, 3-step guided process. Whether you are launching a brand-new curriculum or setting up a standard class section, the wizard helps you lay a strong foundation for your students. Follow this guide to get started.

## Step 1: Access the Dashboard & Start Course Creation
First, log into your instructor or administrator account. In your **Dashboard**, locate your active courses or navigation menu.

To initiate the course creation process, click **Courses** in the sidebar, then click the **"New Course"** button to open the creation wizard.

![Lextures Dashboard](/docs-create-course-dashboard.png)

> [!TIP]
> Course creation permissions are typically granted to Instructors and Global Admins. If you don't see the option to create a course, contact your institution's system administrator.

---

## Step 2: Configure Course Basics
The first step of the wizard asks for the core details of your course.

In the **Basics** section:
1. Enter a clear and descriptive **Course Title** (e.g., *Introduction to Web Development*).
2. (Optional) Provide a brief **Description** to make the course page inviting.
3. Choose a **Course structure** — **Traditional** (standard outline with publish dates) or **Competency-based** (sequential unlock between modules).

![Course Basics Form](/docs-create-course-step1.png)

---

## Step 3: Choose a Syllabus Template
Next, pick a syllabus scaffold to get a head start on your course outline.

The **Syllabus template** step offers several pre-built templates:
- **Blank syllabus** — start from scratch
- **K–12** — term dates, grading categories, and contact info
- **Higher ed** — learning outcomes, weekly rhythm, and campus policies
- **Self-paced** — goals, pacing, and learner milestones
- **Bootcamp / intensive** — daily flow and project-based structure
- **Internal onboarding** — role expectations and first-week checklist

![Syllabus Template Picker](/docs-create-course-step2.png)

> [!NOTE]
> You can fully edit or replace your syllabus at any time from the course's Syllabus page after creation.

---

## Step 4: Add Your First Module
The final step lets you name your first **Module** before opening the course.

Modules are the building blocks of your Lextures course, grouping pages, assignments, and quizzes into logical units (e.g., *Week 1: Introduction & Syllabus*).

You can set a title here, or click **Skip module** to build your module hierarchy once the course shell is ready.

![Module Setup Step](/docs-create-course-step3.png)

---

## Step 5: Start Teaching!
Once you complete the wizard, you'll be taken directly to your newly created course home page!

![New Course Landing Page](/docs-create-course-success.png)

From here, you are ready to:
- **Add Learning Content**: Upload PDFs, embed instructional videos, or write text instructions.
- **Create Assignments & Quizzes**: Set up gradebooks, point distributions, and due dates.
- **Enroll Students & Invite Co-Teachers**: Publish the course and welcome your students to their new learning space.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> By default, newly created courses are in a **Draft** state. Remember to click **"Publish Course"** in your course settings when you are ready to make the course visible to enrolled students.
